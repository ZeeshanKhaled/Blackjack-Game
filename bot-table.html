How Cards Work

2

Next player adds 2 to the draw stack.

3–6

No special effect.

7

You get another turn immediately.

8

Next player must answer with 8 if they can, or they are skipped.

9

No special effect.

10

In 1v1, acts as a reverse and gives the player who used it another turn.

J♠ / J♣

Blackjack: adds 7 to the draw stack.

J♥ / J♦

Redjack: cancels the full draw stack.

Q / K

No special effect.

A

Always playable. You choose the next suit.

Joker

Wildcard. You choose the exact card it is pretending to be.

Knock

Arms your next turn as your attempt to go out.
